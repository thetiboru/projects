What to Watch

The Naruto franchise has a wide variety of different shows and movies. Here's your guide to all the Naruto shows and the order they should be watched in for the best experience.

Naruto

Naruto is the show where the Naruto storyline begins. The story starts off with Naruto and his two comrades Sasuke and Sakura joining Team 7 along with team leader Kakashi. This story mostly documents the journeys of team 7.

Naruto Shippuden

Naruto Shippuden continues where Naruto left off after a 2-year time skip. During this time skip Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura spent time training with each of the three legendary sanin. The plot of this show is no longer about the adventures of team 7 and becomes more focused on defeating the villain group called the "Akatsuki".

Boruto: Naruto Next Generations

Boruto is a show that no longer centers around the main character Naruto but is still a continuation of the Naruto franchise. Boruto, the son of Hokage Naruto, is a young ninja trying to make it out from his father's shadow.

Rock Lee & His Ninja Pals

Rock Lee & His Ninja Pals is a spinoff series that does not have a specific place in the Naruto timeline. This show is a standalone meaning you do not have to watch any of the other Naruto shows beforehand. In this show, we learn about Rock Lee's training and how he overcomes not being able to use a technique known as ninjitsu.
