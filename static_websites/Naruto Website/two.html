Meet the Main Characters

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto, the main protagonist of the show, is an orphan from the Hidden Leaf Village. He was born with the power of the nine-tailed fox given to him by his late father. He joins team 7 with his comrades Sasuke, and Sakura once he graduates the ninja academy. His goal is to become the Hokage one day.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is the secondary protagonist of the series and the rival of Naruto. He is the last remaining member of the Uchiha clan after his brother left and massacred the entire clan. His goal is to seek revenge against his brother.

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi, also known as the "Copy Ninja" is one of the main characters of Naruto and the leader of Team 7. Kakashi is one of the Hidden Leaf's most talented ninja as he possesses a Sharingan, a special kind of eye, throughout most of the show.

Orochimaru

Orochimaru is the primary antagonist of the show Naruto. His goal is to learn as many ninja jutsus as possible. He has the power to steal other people's bodies making him immortal. His objective at the start of the series is to kidnap Sasuke for this purpose.
