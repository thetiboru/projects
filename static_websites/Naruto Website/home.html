Naruto World

Welcome to the world of Naruto!

Naruto is one of my favorite shows of all time! Watching new episodes and rewatching old episodes has been a hobby for me ever since I was a kid. This website will explain to you what the show is about, the history of the show, and the different shows that make up the story!

Synopsis

Naruto is a story about a young ninja pursuing recognition from his village in hopes of one day becoming Hokage (the village leader). Naruto's story is told in two parts, the first part takes place in his pre-teen years, and the second part in his teenage years.

Characters

Naruto characters are very unique and based on Japanese culture. Most characters belong in groups, such as teams in the Hidden Lead village or family-based clans. Most villains in the show also belong/belonged to some of these clans/groups. To learn more click here.

History

The idea of Naruto was created by Masashi Kishimoto in 1996. However, the story was changed multiple times before it was published. The original projects featured Naruto as a chef and a boy that could turn into a fox. To learn more click here.

Shows

The Naruto storyline features four shows, three of which are cannon (shows that relate to the original story). These four shows are Naruto, Naruto Shippuden, Rock Lee & His Ninja Pals, and Boruto Naruto Next Generations. To learn more about these shows click here.
